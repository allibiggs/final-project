Wondering how to get your hands on some half pounders?

Here's the deets.

Check for the drop.

The best way to know when orders are open is to check out our Instagram. The date is also listed at the very top of the Home page. You know, the big scrolling banner.

Once the drop is open, a link to order will show up on the Home page (trust us, you won't be able to miss it.) Orders open about every two weeks.

Choose your flavors.

This is the easy part. We're constantly updating our cookies and coming up with cool new flavors for you to enjoy. Take a look at our IG or our Cookies page for a complete list of what's available.

Cookies are sold individually, and there's no limit to how many you can order. Go crazy.

Go get 'em.

Choose the best option for you during checkout:

  1. Pickup at any Revolution Ice Cream location around Tampa Bay. Grab a pint of Matt's Fatty Rolls while you're at it.
  2. Local delivery: We'll hand deliver this precious cargo right to your door, wrapped in a big bow.
  3. Shipping: Now offered nationwide, for thicc bois from Jersey to the Golden State.
